# Empathy Map

## User
- Government Disaster Management Authority
- Local Communities
- Farmers
- Weather Monitoring Department

## Says
- We need early flood warnings.
- Flood prediction should be accurate.
- We want to protect lives and property.

## Thinks
- Will flooding occur today?
- Is the prediction reliable?
- How can we prepare in advance?

## Does
- Monitors weather reports.
- Collects rainfall and river level data.
- Issues evacuation alerts.

## Feels
- Concerned during heavy rainfall.
- Responsible for public safety.
- Needs quick and accurate decisions.

## Pain Points
- Late flood warnings.
- Inaccurate predictions.
- Lack of real-time monitoring.

## Gains
- Early warning system.
- Better disaster planning.
- Reduced damage and loss of life.
