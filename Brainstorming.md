# Brainstorming

## Project Title
Rising Water Level Prediction Using AI/ML

## Problem
Floods caused by rising water levels result in loss of lives, property, crops, and infrastructure. Current warning systems are often delayed and cannot accurately predict water level increases.

## Proposed Solution
Develop an Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning system that predicts future water levels using rainfall, temperature, humidity, river flow, and historical water level data.

## Objectives
- Predict rising water levels.
- Provide early flood warnings.
- Help disaster management authorities.
- Reduce damage caused by floods.

## Target Users
- Government Authorities
- Disaster Management Teams
- Farmers
- Local Communities
- Environmental Agencies

## Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Scikit-learn
- Flask
- Matplotlib
- GitHub

## Expected Outcome
An AI-powered web application that predicts rising water levels and warns users about possible flood risks.
