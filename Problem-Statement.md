# Problem Statement

## Title
AI-Based Rising Water Level Prediction System

## Problem

Floods are one of the most destructive natural disasters. Rising water levels caused by heavy rainfall and changing weather conditions lead to loss of lives, damage to infrastructure, and economic losses. Existing flood warning systems often fail to provide timely and accurate predictions.

## Proposed Solution

Develop a Machine Learning-based prediction system that analyzes historical weather and water level data to predict future water levels. The system provides early warnings so authorities and communities can prepare before flooding occurs.

## Objectives

- Predict rising water levels accurately.
- Provide early flood alerts.
- Reduce flood-related damage.
- Support disaster management.

## Expected Outcome

An AI-powered web application that predicts rising water levels and helps authorities make timely decisions.
